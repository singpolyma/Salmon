What is this?

This is a proof of concept demonstration for the Salmon Protocol. Salmon aims to stop fragmenting conversations across the web. The protocol defines how comments can swim back upstream to the resource being discussed. It's open, standards based, decentralized, abuse resistant, and user centric. How's that for buzzwords?

To try it out, go peek at the demonstration aggregator and reply to a post in it.
